01 — Signature

A practitioner first, an operator always.

I'm Maulik — Director of Operations at Tripearlsoft, a branding & digital studio based in Ahmedabad. My job is to keep strategy, design and engineering moving in the same direction so every project we touch finishes louder than it began.

I work across websites, brand systems, mobile apps and digital marketing — usually with founders, small teams, and operators who need a partner that can think in roadmaps and execute in pixels.

Outside the work itself, I care about three things: relationships that outlast a single brief, process that respects the client's time, and numbers at the end of every quarter.

03 — Approach

How good work actually gets made.

01 / 05

Listen.

A first meeting is for diagnosis — not a sales pitch. We map your goals, audience and constraints before anyone opens Figma.

→
02 / 05

Frame.

One page. The story, the success metric, the non-negotiables. If the brief can't fit on one page, it isn't ready to start.

→
03 / 05

Make.

Design and engineering in parallel, with the client in the room weekly. We share work-in-progress; we don't disappear into reveals.

→
04 / 05

Ship.

QA across devices, performance audits, content checks — and a launch plan with paid & organic switched on in the same week.

→
05 / 05

Compound.

The first 90 days after launch decide everything. We stay on, measure, iterate — and turn the launch into a growth flywheel.
